# Phase 1 — Business Recommendations

Based on the revenue diagnostics, the following high-level recommendations 
are proposed to improve revenue predictability, margin performance, and 
customer engagement.

---

## 1. Strengthen Q4 Inventory and Marketing Strategy
Given the recurring revenue peaks in September–December:
- Increase inventory for high-demand SKUs  
- Expand Q4-focused digital promotions  
- Align sales incentives with seasonal surges  

---

## 2. Improve January–February Retention Through Targeted Campaigns
To counter post-holiday decline:
- Launch loyalty renewal offers  
- Introduce “Start the Year Smart” bundles  
- Provide targeted promotions to high-churn risk customers  

---

## 3. Reassess Pricing and Discounting Strategy for Furniture
Furniture shows:
- High revenue  
- Very low profitability  

Actions:
- Rationalize discount levels  
- Review supplier contracts  
- Prioritize high-margin Furniture SKUs in promotions  

---

## 4. Category Portfolio Optimization
Insights show:
- Technology is consistently profitable  
- Office Supplies offers stable margins  
- Furniture is margin-negative in many months  

Recommendation:
- Shift marketing and placement budget toward high-margin categories  
- Conduct a detailed cost-to-serve analysis for Furniture  

---

## 5. Build Forecasting and Stock Allocation Around Peak Months
Given predictable seasonality:
- Use historical Q4 trends for demand forecasting  
- Pre-order inventory for Technology and Office Supplies  
- Design promotional calendars aligned with high-performing months  

---

## 6. Focus Operational Readiness on High-Volume Periods
Revenue spikes imply pressure on logistics and fulfillment:
- Scale warehouse staffing during Q4  
- Optimize delivery routes for high-order periods  
- Prepare for surge-demand scenarios

---

These recommendations directly tie business strategy to insights produced 
in SQL analysis and mirror the framework used in analytics consulting roles 
such as ZS DAA, Tiger Analytics, Fractal, and Deloitte AI/Insights teams.
